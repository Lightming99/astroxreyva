✨ Easy Order Placement Process

Our streamlined 6-step process ensures a seamless experience from consultation to delivery

1

Share Your Requirements

Tell us your desired gemstone type, metal preference, karatage, design style, and budget range. Our gemstone experts will carefully understand your vision, astrological needs, and personal preferences to recommend the most suitable options for you.

2

Gemstone Selection & Verification

We showcase handpicked, certified natural gemstones that match your specifications through high-resolution photos, detailed videos, or live video calls. Each gemstone comes with proper certification (GIA/IGI) and complete transparency about origin, treatments, and quality parameters.

3

Design Selection & Customization

Choose your preferred gemstone and explore our exclusive jewelry design catalog. We offer both traditional and contemporary settings, with options for complete customization to create a unique piece that perfectly complements your chosen gemstone.

4

CAD Design & Detailed Quotation

Once you finalize the gemstone, design, and metal specifications, our designers create a detailed CAD (Computer-Aided Design) rendering of your piece. We provide a comprehensive quotation including gemstone cost, metal weight, making charges, and all applicable taxes with complete price transparency.

5

Expert Manufacturing

After your approval and payment confirmation, our master craftsmen and skilled artisans begin manufacturing your custom piece. We use traditional techniques combined with modern precision to ensure exceptional quality, durability, and attention to every detail.

6

Quality Check & Secure Delivery

Your completed jewelry undergoes thorough quality inspection before secure packaging. We ensure safe, insured delivery within 12-15 business days directly to your doorstep. Each piece comes with authenticity certificates and detailed care instructions.
